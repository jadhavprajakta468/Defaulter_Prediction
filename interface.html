About The Project

This project aims to predict whether college students will be in the defaulter list based on their attendance. It utilizes machine learning techniques, specifically the Random Forest algorithm, to analyze attendance data and make predictions.

Random Forest is an ensemble learning method that constructs multiple decision trees during training and outputs the mode of the classes (classification) or mean prediction (regression) of the individual trees. It is widely used in various machine learning tasks due to its robustness and accuracy.
